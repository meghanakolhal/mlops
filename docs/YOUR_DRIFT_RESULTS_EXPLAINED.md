**Your Data Comparison:**

- `text`: Mix of various ticket types

- `customer_tier`: More Bronze customers
- `text`: Different types of tickets (more web-based, more phone urgent issues)

**The Math:**
```
Email tickets:
- Reference: 40% of 500 = 200 tickets
- New: 5% of 20 = 1 ticket
- Change: 40% ‚Üí 5% = 35 percentage point drop!

Web tickets:
- Reference: 30% of 500 = 150 tickets  
- New: 60% of 20 = 12 tickets
- Change: 30% ‚Üí 60% = 30 percentage point increase!
```

**Statistical Test Result:**
- Chi-square test compares these distributions
- P-value < 0.05 = Statistically significant (not random)
- **Even with 20 rows, such dramatic changes are detected!**

**Statistical Tests Are Designed for This:**

1. **Chi-square Test** (for categorical columns like `source`, `customer_tier`):

   - Detects **distribution changes**, not absolute counts
   - Your change: 40% ‚Üí 5% email is **huge** even with 20 rows

2. **Kolmogorov-Smirnov Test** (for numeric/text columns):
   - Works with 20+ samples
   - Compares **distributions**, not individual values
   - Detects shape changes in distributions
